FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Find Answers to Common Questions

We've compiled answers to the most frequently asked questions about our platform and services.

×

General Questions

What is this platform about?

Our platform provides mental health resources, community support, and self-care tools to help users improve their well-being.

Is this platform free to use?

Yes! Access to our resources, community discussions, and self-help tools is completely free.

Do I need an account to use the platform?

You can browse public resources, but signing up gives access to personalized activities, community discussions, and exclusive content.

Privacy & Safety

Is my information private?

Yes! We prioritize your privacy, and you can choose to remain anonymous in discussions.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. You can delete your account anytime in the Profile Settings section.

What should I do if I see harmful or inappropriate content?

Use the Report button to notify our moderation team, and we'll take action.

Community & Support

How do I join the community discussions?

Simply sign up and start engaging in forum discussions or share your experiences.

Can I post anonymously in the community?

Yes! You can choose to hide your identity when posting.

Is this platform a substitute for therapy?

No. While we provide self-help tools and peer support, our platform does not replace professional therapy. If you're struggling, we encourage reaching out to a licensed therapist.

Self-Help Tools & Resources

How do I use the self-care tools?

Go to the Self-Help Tools section, where you'll find journaling prompts, mindfulness exercises, and stress management worksheets.

Where can I find mental health articles and videos?

Visit our Resources section for expert-written articles, guided meditations, and mental health webinars.

Account Help

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it.

How can I change my username or profile picture?

Go to Profile Settings and update your information.

Can I suggest new features or improvements?

Absolutely! Contact us via our contact form, and we'd love to hear your ideas.

Still Have Questions?

If your question isn't listed here, feel free to contact us or ask in the community!
