General Questions

What is this platform about?

Our platform provides mental health resources, community support, and self-care tools to help users improve their well-being.

Is this platform free to use?

Yes! Access to our resources, community discussions, and self-help tools is completely free.

Do I need an account to use the platform?

You can browse public resources, but signing up gives access to personalized activities, community discussions, and exclusive content.

Privacy & Safety

Is my information private?

Yes! We prioritize your privacy, and you can choose to remain anonymous in discussions.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. You can delete your account anytime in the Profile Settings section.

What should I do if I see harmful or inappropriate content?

Use the Report button to notify our moderation team, and we'll take action.
